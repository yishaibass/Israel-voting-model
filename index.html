The matching challenge

Each ballot box needs a demographic profile. CBS publishes a file linking ballot boxes to statistical areas — but two complications arise:

Split boxes

When a polling station gets too many registered voters, the CEC splits it (box 3 becomes 3.1, 3.2, 3.3). CBS only records the original integer (3). Resolved by mapping decimal sub-boxes back to their integer parent.

SA boundary changes

CBS updated its statistical area map between 2011 and 2022. A conversion file bridges the two vintages. Where a 2011 SA split into multiple 2022 SAs, the ballot box is assigned to the largest 2022 fragment by population.
